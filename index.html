Concept of the collection

For cut and silhouette, then I intend to use negative space from collages and my own physical form exploration. As part of a textile narrative, a range of my photographs featuring different textures will be used. I tend to use knitting, weaving, and tweed techniques for my collection, making textiles my primary focus. I'm still uncertain about the seams and finishing, but I'm considering keeping it neat and classy.

Overall, the project combines the destructiveness of the urban environment and abandonment in the form of certain objects and also addresses the inner abandoned and forsaken feelings.

The first look explores fragmentation through asymmetry, layering, and contrasting knitted textures. The interaction between soft crochet, transparent mesh, and distorted proportions reflects the uneven and constantly shifting surfaces of the urban environment.

Hello! My name is Vin Shturmer, and I am a fashion and textile artist based in Moscow. My practice explores the relationship between personal experience, surrounding environments, and material experimentation. I am particularly interested in textile development, deconstruction, unconventional materials, and the transformation of familiar forms.
